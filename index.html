About BlackVault

        

          A disguised notepad that unlocks a hidden encrypted vault when you 
          tap the trash icon 6 times or save a note with the trap phrase.
        

        
    
          
  • Each secure note is encrypted locally (AES-GCM).
    • 
          
  • Passcode derives the key via PBKDF2; nothing leaves your device.
    • 
          
  • “BURN ALL” wipes every secure note and your public note.
    • 
        

      

    

  


  
  

    

      
Delete ALL notes?

      

        This will permanently delete all secure notes and clear the public note.
        This cannot be undone.
